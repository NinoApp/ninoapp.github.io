Introducing Smart Notes

Ever took an image of someting you would like to use as material? Biggest problem one might face is that an image is just that, an image. No way to change it, incorporate it into your existing work, nothing.

Smart Note takes an image and turns it into a fully customizable one. Smart Note extracts text and makes it editable through an comprehensive editor. It finds the images & makes them modifiable and turns math equations into editable Latex format. Combined with Smart Note feature, you may automatically generate questions of any book page.
